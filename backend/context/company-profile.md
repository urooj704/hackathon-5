# FlowForge — Company Profile

## Overview
FlowForge is a no-code workflow automation platform that enables businesses to connect their apps, automate repetitive processes, and build internal tools — without writing a single line of code. Think of us as the automation layer between your SaaS stack.

**Founded:** 2021
**Headquarters:** Austin, TX (remote-first)
**Team size:** ~180 employees
**Stage:** Series B ($42M raised, October 2024)
**Customers:** 4,200 paying accounts across 60+ countries

## Core Product Pillars

1. **Visual Workflow Builder** — Drag-and-drop canvas with conditional branching, loops, and delay nodes
2. **Integration Hub** — 300+ pre-built connectors (Slack, Gmail, HubSpot, Salesforce, Stripe, Notion, Airtable, GitHub, etc.)
3. **Custom Webhooks** — Incoming and outgoing webhooks for any service not in the Hub
4. **Data Transformer** — JSON/CSV parsing, formula fields, field mapping between apps
5. **Error Handling & Retry Logic** — Automatic retries, dead-letter queues, failure notifications
6. **Team Workspaces** (Business+) — Shared workflow library, role-based access, comments
7. **FlowForge AI** (Beta) — Natural language workflow creation, smart field suggestions

---

## Key Differentiators vs Competitors (Zapier, Make.com)

- **Faster execution:** Sub-second trigger latency vs. 15-minute polling on Zapier's free tier
- **Better error visibility:** Every failed task shows exact failure point with raw payload
- **Cheaper at scale:** Task-based pricing is ~40% cheaper than Zapier at equivalent volume
- **No "Zap steps" gotchas:** Multi-step workflows count as one task regardless of step count (on Business+)

## Support Structure

- **Starter:** Community forum + in-app help docs (no live support)
- **Growth:** Email support (target response: 24h business hours)
- **Business:** Priority email + live chat (target response: 4h, M–F 9am–6pm CT)
- **Enterprise:** Dedicated CSM + Slack Connect + 2h SLA

**Support team size:** 12 agents (CS agents) + 2 CSMs for enterprise accounts
**Current ticket volume:** ~350 tickets/day
**Peak hours:** 9am–12pm CT and 2pm–5pm CT

## Current Pain Points (Internal)

- Support volume grew 3x in 6 months but team only grew 1.5x
- 40% of tickets are repetitive "how do I" questions answerable from docs
- WhatsApp channel launched 3 months ago — inconsistent response quality
- No unified customer view: email ↔ WhatsApp ↔ web tickets are siloed
- First-response time slipped from 3h → 8h avg on Growth plan accounts
